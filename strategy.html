Parametric Shed Automation

1-Page Executive Summary

We are not building a shed configurator. We are building a parametric design and cost engine that encodes structural logic, material rules, planning constraints, manufacturable outputs, and cost structure.

The shed is the first application. The system is the long-term asset.

Sequencing Plan
1
Current Phase
Internal Optimisation

Use the engine to strengthen our core building business. This internal advantage funds everything else.

  • Faster and more accurate quoting
  • Fewer design and build errors
  • Clearer client conversations
  • Reduced cognitive load
  • Higher close rates
  • Improved margin protection
2
Next
Structured Cost Transparency

Introduce controlled cost clarity. Instead of opaque pricing, clients see:

  • Materials
  • Labour
  • Overheads
  • Trade-offs between options

The system ensures no structurally invalid configurations, no underspec'd builds, no hidden compromises. Price becomes a consequence of choices, not a negotiation tool.

We are not racing to the bottom. We are reframing value around informed decisions.

3
Future
Controlled Supplier & Labour Competition

The engine can eventually host multiple material suppliers and defined labour providers. Suppliers compete inside strict parametric constraints:

  • Grade
  • Lead time
  • Reliability
  • Cost bands

Because the system defines what a "valid build" is, quality cannot degrade through competition. We own the rules. Others operate within them.

This is not an open marketplace. It is a constrained, rules-based ecosystem.

4
Optional
Platform Licensing

License the parametric engine to other builders or industries. The system — not the shed — is the product.

💰
Strategic Margin Shift

Traditional builder margin relies partly on opacity. Our model shifts margin to:

  • System ownership
  • Decision compression
  • Error reduction
  • Speed
  • Risk elimination
  • Preset expertise
We stop selling

"A shed."

We start selling

"A resolved, de-risked decision."

🤖
Role of AI

AI is not a gimmick feature. It functions as an optimisation layer, a trade-off explainer, and a filter through complexity. AI reduces cognitive burden, not adds to it.

"Optimise for longevity under £X"
"Minimise lifetime cost"
"What changes if insulation increases?"
🛡️
Risks & Guardrails

Risks:

  • Premature full transparency
  • Uncontrolled supplier marketplace
  • Loss of strategic focus

Guardrails:

  • Layered rollout
  • Invite-only supplier participation
  • Maintain internal advantage before external exposure
🎯
Strategic Principle
"We are not building a shed marketplace. We are encoding what a good shed is and allowing competition only inside that encoded truth."

The system defines quality. That system is the competitive advantage.
